Understanding the Requirement

What Is MS ISO 37001:3016 Anti-Bribery Management System?

MS ISO 37001:2016 is Malaysia's adopted international standard for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and improving an Anti-Bribery Management System (ABMS). It provides a structured framework to help organisations prevent, detect, and respond to bribery.

The Standard Requires
  • Anti-Bribery Policy Endorsed by top management, covering all business functions and third parties
  • Corruption Risk Assessment Identifying, analysing and evaluating bribery risks across the organisation
  • Due Diligence Procedures for sub-contractors, suppliers, business associates, and high-risk employees
  • Whistleblowing Mechanism A safe, confidential channel to report suspected bribery
  • Documentation & Records For auditing and continuous improvement, aligned with MACC Act 2009
Why It Matters for G7

Beyond SPKK compliance, ABMS certification signals to government clients, project owners, and business partners that your organisation operates with integrity and accountability. It aligns with Malaysia's national anti-corruption framework and MACC Act 2009 obligations.

G7
All Grade G7 contractors must comply — no exceptions, effective January 2027
G7 Contractor Obligations

What G7 Contractors Need to Complete

To achieve MS ISO 37001 certification, G7 contractors must establish and demonstrate the following in a formal third-party audit by an accredited certification body.

  • 01
    Establish a formal Anti-Bribery Policy endorsed by top management, covering all business functions and third parties.
  • 02
    Conduct a Corruption Risk Assessment — identify, analyse, and evaluate bribery risks across the whole organisation.
  • 03
    Implement Due Diligence procedures for sub-contractors, suppliers, business associates, and high-risk employees.
  • 04
    Set up a Whistleblowing Mechanism — a safe, confidential channel to report suspected bribery internally and externally.
  • 05
    Maintain Documentation & Records for auditing and continuous improvement, aligned with MACC Act 2009.
  • 06
    Pass a Certification Audit by an accredited certification body to receive the MS37001 certificate.
